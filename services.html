Engineering Services We Offer

End-to-end engineering solutions for critical infrastructure projects

Fabrication, Piping & Structural Works

Our fabrication services encompass the complete spectrum of structural steel works, precision piping, and mechanical installations for petroleum facilities and industrial plants.

  • Underground pipeline installation and dismantling (HSD, SKO, MS pipelines)
  • Non-space pump (NSP) fabrication and commissioning
  • Tank conversion and modification works
  • Custom fabrication of dispensing systems
  • Structural steel erection and canopy works
  • Pedestal casting and manhole alterations

Fire Safety Equipment & Systems

Comprehensive fire safety solutions including equipment supply, installation, testing, and annual maintenance contracts for industrial facilities.

  • Emergency response system setup
  • Regular maintenance and inspection services

Civil & Painting Services

Professional civil construction, infrastructure upgradation, and industrial painting services tailored for petroleum facilities.
