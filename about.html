About Suyesh Engineering

Home About Us

30 Years of Engineering Excellence

Founded in 1994 by Mr. Shivaji Sangale, Suyesh Engineering has grown from a local contractor to become one of the most trusted MSME-certified engineering firms in Western India. Our journey spans three decades of delivering precision-engineered solutions for critical infrastructure projects in the oil & gas sector.

As a registered MSME micro enterprise (UDYAM-MH-18-0084813), we specialize in comprehensive fabrication, piping, structural works, civil construction, and fire safety solutions. Our partnership with Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL) as an approved vendor (Code: 304733) stands as a testament to our technical capabilities and commitment to excellence.

With an impressive compound annual growth rate of 48.7% and a turnover reaching ₹1.88 Crores in FY 2023-24, we combine financial stability with technical expertise. Our debt-free operations and current ratio of 3.45 reflect our prudent financial management and sustainable business practices.
